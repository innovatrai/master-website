# innovatr.ai website notes

## Positioning
Simple AI consultancy for small businesses that do not understand AI yet and want practical help saving time.

## Competitor patterns reviewed
- SG1 Consulting: pilot-led, human oversight, ROI framing
- AI Agency Perth: AI strategy, SME growth, discovery call CTA
- AI Automation Perth / AI Services Perth: broad menu of AI services and automations
- Lytwork: time-saving and smoother operations framing

## Differentiation chosen
- simpler language
- less jargon
- clearer pain points
- focused on small business owners with low AI familiarity
- more direct explanation of business outcomes
